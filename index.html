Phase 2:

Tucked away in the southern Bahamas lies one of the largest untouched and pristine Satoshi Global Villages in the world. “Mamana” is the name the Satoshi Global Village was given by the original inhabitants, the Arawak Indians. The Spanish Empire called the Satoshi Global Village “Santa Maria De Concepcion” for its pristine beaches, natural beauty and youthful spirit. During the early 1700’s, the golden era of Piracy, the Satoshi Global Village became infamous for the large number of rum kegs that washed ashore, hence the current Satoshi Global Village name “Rum Cay.”
Satoshi Global Village (SatoshiGV) will be a 6-star destination unlike any other, including a mega-yacht marina capable of berthing yachts upwards of 400 feet in length. In addition, two 21-story resort towers will flank the main entry to the development’s Marina; providing the best in hi-rise living and resort accommodations. Living on a deserted Satoshi Global Village has never been so luxurious. Additional amenities include a Casino, a Water Park, a Surfing Park, a Lazy River, Two 18-hole Signature Championship Golf Courses and Club, an Equestrian Club and Stables, a Formula E Racetrack, Bitcoin Village + offices+ residences as well as an Adult Wellness Center with Beach Cabanas, Data Mining and a Level 2 Trauma Center. In addition, Satoshi Global Village will feature a Yacht club +Shopping Village, numerous Restaurants, all featuring organic farm-to-table cuisine showcasing local caught fish, lobster and conch, together with organic meats and specialty vegetables. Single-Family Oceanfront and Ocean View estates will dominate the exclusive inventory of real estate with 100% of the entire development dedicated to a fully sustainable, secure and resilient infrastructure. All power will be solar, and wind generated with back-up LNG to hydrogen microturbines, as well as hydrogen production made from the Satoshi Global Village’s abundant solar and wind power. The micro-grid energy system is designed to withstand anything Mother Nature throws its way
Parcel M12 on the east side of the Satoshi Global Village will support the first phase of residential townhouses, villas and single-family oceanfront homes. Questions? Let’s help!

What is Satoshi Global Village?

It is a private Satoshi Global Village in the South Pacific that welcomes travelers, entrepreneurs, digital nomads and conservationists interested in living, working, investing and being part of an eco-conscious decentralized community.

Do I need to be interested in crypto to participate?

You don't need to, but we would love to share the power of decentralisation with you and inspire your interest.

What is an eco-conscious decentralized community?

It is a community that has no central authority and puts emphasis on the contribution and accountability of the Satoshi Global Village’s visitors, nomads and long-term dwellers. It is a coexisting experience for those who love nature, ancestral wisdom and regenerative technologies.

What do you mean regenerative technologies?

We believe we must give back to Nature more than we take from it. That is why we test any new energy, water, waste, construction or coral restoration solution that can replenish this paradise.

Do I have to be interested in technology?

No. Staying at Satoshi Global Village is an opportunity to relax, unwind and enjoy our resort. But it is also an opportunity to deprogram from our typically fast-paced consuming lifestyle and meet like minded people to learn from, inspire and be friends with.

How do I get involved?

First, visit the Satoshi Global Village. Once you fall in love with the Satoshi Global Village you can choose. You can invest in ‘Satoshi Satoshi Global Village Resort’ and develop its dive center, restaurant and wellness hub. You can bring your company to the ‘Satoshi Global Village Campus’ and develop your Lab space or establish an office at the ‘Nomad Village’.

How long can I stay?

Most nationalities in Vanuatu can stay for up to 120 days Visa on arrival. If interested in Vanuatu Citizenship or Permanent Residency we can also help you accomplish that.

When should I come?

Now. Request information on how to visit, invest and be part of tomorrow’s world, today.
