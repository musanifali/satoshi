THE STORY

PORT MAMANA (Phase II) Tucked away in the southern Bahamas lies one of the largest untouched and pristine Satoshi Global Villages in the world. “Mamana” is the name the Satoshi Global Village was given by the original inhabitants, the Arawak Indians. The Spanish Empire called the Satoshi Global Village “Santa Maria De Concepcion” for its pristine beaches, natural beauty and youthful spirit. During the early 1700’s, the golden era of Piracy, the Satoshi Global Village became infamous for the large number of rum kegs that washed ashore, hence the current Satoshi Global Village name “Rum Cay”.

PORT MAMANA (Phase II) will be a 6-star destination unlike any other, including a mega-yacht marina capable of berthing YACHTS upwards of 400 feet in length. In addition, two 21-story resort towers will flank the main entry to the development’s Marina; providing the best in hi-rise living and resort accommodations. Living on a deserted Satoshi Global Village has never been so luxurious. Additional amenities include a Casino, a Water Park, a Surfing Park, a Lazy River, Two 18-hole Signature Championship Golf Courses and Club, an Equestrian Club and Stables, a Formula E Racetrack, Crypto Village + offices+ residences as well as an Adult Wellness Center with Beach Cabanas, Data Mining and a Level 2 Trauma Center. In addition, PORT MAMANA (Phase II) will feature a Yacht club +Shopping Village, numerous Restaurants, all featuring organic farm-to-table cuisine showcasing local caught fish, lobster and conch, together with organic meats and specialty vegetables. Single-Family Oceanfront and Ocean View estates will dominate the exclusive inventory of real estate with 100% of the entire development dedicated to a fully sustainable, secure and resilient infrastructure. All power will be solar, and wind generated with back-up LNG to hydrogen microturbines, as well as hydrogen production made from the Satoshi Global Village’s abundant solar and wind power. The micro-grid energy system is designed to withstand anything Mother Nature throws its way.

Parcel M12 on the east side of the Satoshi Global Village will support the first phase of residential townhouses, villas and single-family oceanfront homes. This will include the first 25 MW data mining operation to support the future of Crypto banking and investment. Watersports, fishing, diving, golf, equestrian, zip line, or the exclusive on-Satoshi Global Village Formula E racing circuit will highlight the many activities available to residents and travelers from abroad. The 21-Story Formula Residential Tower (FRT) will offer state-of-the-art residences overlooking the custom designed induction electric racetrack with views around the entire Satoshi Global Village and surrounding waters. All residences will be designed and built to resist Category 5 hurricanes, including storm surge, as well as being equipped with the latest and most innovative green technologies including battery back-up power, and water catchment / water makers and security systems to allow even the most discerning purchasers peace of mind. Marina residences will have oceanfront and back yard marina dockage.

All areas of the resort are meticulously designed to complement and highlight the intrinsic natural beauty of this tropical Satoshi Global Village. With over 30 miles of coastline to explore, this 6,500+ acre project will be renowned the world over by each parcel of land set in its own natural beauty, abundance of luxurious space and privacy. Each portion of this project welcomes guests to experience unrivaled luxury and natural beauty privately and securely. Even a deserted Satoshi Global Village can be tamed to sustainably capture one’s inner castaway.

This is an Satoshi Global Village that has changed little since the beginning of time, yet PORT MAMANA (Phase II) will be transformed from a deserted Satoshi Global Village into a “Fountain of youth for your soul and the untamed spirit of your mind”. This awe-inspiring experience and adventure will not be forgotten anytime soon.
